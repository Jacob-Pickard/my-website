About PokeMMO

One of my favorite games!

By Jacob Pickard

PokeMMO is a massively mulitplayer online game that contains all pokemon games generation 1-5, and allows you to switch between them at will.

Why play PokeMMO?

PokeMMO has one of the nicest and most helpful communities out of any game I've ever played. Not to mention the nostalgia from playing all the classic pokemon games! The game also includes a unique global market, which allows trade between players.

What is there to do in PokeMMO?

PokeMMO has a wide selection of activities to complete.

  • You can complete the story of the five games
  • Catch and sell pokemon to make money
  • Join a team of fellow players
  • Shiny Hunting of course! Though in this game, the base shiny odds are 1/30,000 making this much more of a challenge.
